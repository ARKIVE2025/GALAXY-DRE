What is DRE Workflow?
DRE Workflow is your project's starting point. Click Plan with AI First — the Orchestrator will ask a few simple questions, understand what you're building, and create a perfect visual step-by-step map for you.
How the Orchestrator Works
1
Understand — Tell the AI what you want to build in plain English. No tech jargon needed.
2
Clarify — It asks a few follow-up questions to understand your goals, audience, and needs.
3
Review — It shows you a plain-English summary of what it understood. You confirm or refine.
4
Build — Only then does it generate your visual workflow. The result is precisely tailored to your project.
