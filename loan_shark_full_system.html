★ USE LOANS AS A TOOL ★
Just because you owe more than your paycheck doesn't mean you're stuck.
With 6+ loan apps, you have something most people don't: options.
This tab shows you how to use your apps in the RIGHT ORDER so each paycheck works harder for you.
🔑 THE BIG IDEA — EXPLAINED SIMPLY Not all loan apps are equal. Some cost you nothing to use (no fees, instant re-borrow). Others cost you $3–$15 every single time. The smart move: use your FREE apps to pay off your EXPENSIVE apps. Then you're only cycling through the free ones — and your total debt stops growing.

Think of it like this: if you had a credit card with 0% interest AND one with 25% interest, you'd pay off the 25% one first. Same idea here.
