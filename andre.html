What We Audit

Six categories where businesses consistently overpay, get locked in, or buy services AI can now replace.

🌐

Web Design & Development

Monthly maintenance fees on static sites, five-figure quotes for basic builds, proprietary CMS lock-in, and redesigns with no measurable outcome tied to them.

"The question is whether what you're paying for is tied to an outcome — or just to keeping the lights on."
📊

CRM & Sales Tools

Features that never get used, overlapping tools doing the same job, per-seat pricing beyond actual team usage, and no clear line between what the tool costs and what it generates.

"If you can't point to a direct revenue outcome from that tool, it's overhead — not infrastructure."
📣

Paid Ads & Media

Agencies paid a percentage of your ad spend — a model that rewards burning more of your budget. No cost-per-acquisition tracked. Ads running to landing pages that were never tested.

"Percentage-of-spend models mean your agency profits more when your budget goes up — regardless of your results."
🔁

SaaS Subscriptions

Auto-renewing tools no one actively uses, enterprise tiers for teams of three, and subscription stacks with 30–40% redundancy baked in because no one did the audit.

"Most businesses are paying for 30–40% more SaaS than they actively use."
🖥️

Hosting & Domains

Premium hosting for sites that serve under a thousand visitors a month. Hosting bundled into agency retainers at two to three times the commodity rate with no visibility.

"Hosting is largely a commodity. If it's bundled into an agency retainer, it's almost always marked up."
🤖

AI-Replaceable Services

Copywriting retainers, chatbot subscriptions, internal knowledge bases, report generation, proposal drafting, and lead qualification flows — all now buildable with a custom AI solution for a fraction of the recurring cost.

"Has anyone shown you what this looks like built with AI? The recurring cost model for this type of work has changed significantly."
The DRE System

Diagnose. Redesign. Complete.

Every consult runs through the same three-stage framework — a structured process for cutting what doesn't belong, validating what does, and building toward a single aligned outcome.

01 — DIAGNOSE
Map the Spend
We identify every active vendor, tool, and retainer — then score each one against a single question: is this tied to a real outcome?
02 — REDESIGN
Cut and Replace
We eliminate redundant costs, flag misaligned contracts, and identify what AI or simpler tooling can replace without losing capability.
03 — COMPLETE
Build to Align
We deliver a clear plan — or build the replacement ourselves — so the budget freed from overhead funds work that actually moves the mission forward.
